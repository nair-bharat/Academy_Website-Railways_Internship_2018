ACHIEVEMENTS & AWARDS


Year: 2012-13
# CRCA sucessfully conducted the IRCC (Dance) 2012-13 at ZRTI Bhusaval. # Artists of CRCA won the First prize for One Act Play - "Ek Doon Adich" at IRCC (Drama) 2012-13 held at Kharaghpur. # Artist of CRCA won the Third prize for Instrumental Music at IRCC (Music) 2012-13 held at Secunderabad. # CRCA sucessfully conducted a Theatre Workshop for Artists and interested employees of Central Railway at CSTM. # Artists of CRCA won prize for Best Acting in "Spandan"at One-Act Play competition organised by The Akhil Bharatiya Naatya Parishad in Mumbai. # Artists of CRCA gave a memorable musical performance on the occasion of the National Rail Museum Foundation day at New Delhi in presence of CRB and other dignitaries. # CRCA staged a marvellous musical programme " Megh Malhar " - a combination of songs and dances based on the monsoon at CSTM.

Year: 2011-12
# CRCA won the 2nd prize for the best One-Act Play "Mayyat" in IRCC (Drama) 2011-12 held at Jaipur.

Year: 2010-11
# CRCA organised " Sanskriti Yatra" a cultural programme based on the cultural heritage of Maharashtra, to welcome the Sanskriti Express at CSTM.

Year: 2007-08
# CRCA presented a musical programme in memory of the great composers, the Late S D Burman and the Late R D Burman.

Year: 2006-07
# CRCA presented " Megh Malhar" a programme on monsoon based songs and dances and " Rang de Basanti" a programme on patriotic songs. # CRCA organised back to back -programme of two One-Act Plays viz "Kaala Suraj" and " Badi Buwaji". # CRCA has also successfully staged a number of plays in the past viz. " Khamosh Adalat Jaari Hai","Shuturmurg" and "Brihannada".
